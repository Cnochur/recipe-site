Asian Inspired Chicken Curry

Picture this: a sizzling dance party in a pot, where tender chunks of chicken (or tofu, for our veggie pals) groove with a rainbow coalition of veggies. They mingle in a coconut milk bath, serenaded by a spicy symphony of aromatic curry paste. It's like a flavor fiesta where every spoonful is an invitation to join the tastebud boogie. Our curry - the spiciest, creamiest, most flavorful bash in town.

Ingredients

  • Protein of choice (chicken, tofu, beef, etc.)
  • Curry Paste (store-bought or homemade mix of spices)
  • Coconut Milk
  • Vegetables (such as bell peppers, onions, carrots, etc.)
  • Aromatics (onions, garlic, ginger)

Method

  1. Prepare the Aromatics and Curry Base:
    - Heat a tablespoon of oil in a pan. Add chopped onions, minced garlic, and grated ginger. Saut√© until fragran
  2. Add Protein and Vegetables:
    - Add your choice of protein and the vegetables, cut into bite-sized pieces. Stir well to coat with the aromatics.
  3. Incorporate the Curry Paste and Coconut Milk:
    - Add 2-3 tablespoons of the curry paste, or as desired for your preferred level of spice. Stir it into the mixture to coat the protein and vegetables.
    - Pour in one can of coconut milk (around 400ml). Stir well to combine and create the curry base.
  4. Simmer and Cook:
    - Allow the mixture to simmer over low heat for around 20-30 minutes, or until the protein and vegetables are cooked through and the flavors have melded.
  5. Final Touches:
    - Taste and adjust the seasoning. Add salt if necessary.
    Serve:
    - Serve the curry with rice or bread of your choice.
