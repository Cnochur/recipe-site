Dumplings

Ready for a flavor jam session? Our dynamite dumplings are like a sold-out rock concert in your mouth! Ground pork and cabbage team up in perfect harmony, creating a flavorful gig that's a hit single on your taste buds. Each bite's a drum solo of deliciousness, hitting all the right notes. Ingredients

    Filling:

  • 1 pound ground pork (or a mixture of pork and shrimp)
  • 2 cups finely chopped Napa cabbage
  • 3-4 green onions, finely chopped
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • Salt and pepper to taste

    • Dumpling Wrapper:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3/4 cup warm water
  • Pinch of salt

Method

  1. Prepare the Filling:
    - In a mixing bowl, combine the ground pork, finely chopped Napa cabbage, green onions, soy sauce, salt, and pepper. Mix until thoroughly combined.
  2. Create the Dough:
    - In a separate bowl, combine the all-purpose flour with a pinch of salt. Gradually add warm water to the flour, mixing until a dough forms.
    - Knead the dough on a floured surface until it's smooth. Cover the dough with a damp cloth and let it rest for about 30 minutes.
  3. Shape and Fill the Dumplings:
    - Take a small piece of the dough and roll it into a thin, circular wrapper using a rolling pin. Ensure the edges are thinner than the center.
    - Place a small amount of filling in the center of each wrapper.
    - Fold the wrapper in half over the filling and pinch the edges to seal the dumplings.
  4. Cooking the Dumplings:

    To steam the dumplings:
    -Place them in a steamer lined with cabbage leaves or parchment paper for about 10-12 minutes.

    To pan-fry the dumplings:
    -Heat oil in a non-stick pan, place the dumplings, and cook until the bottoms are golden brown.
    -Then, add a small amount of water and cover to steam until the water evaporates.
  5. Serve:
    - Serve the dumplings hot with a dipping sauce made from soy sauce, vinegar, and a touch of chili oil if desired.
