Beef 'nd Broccoli

Beef and broccoli: the culinary tag team that's all about bringing harmony to your plate. It's a dish where the beef rocks the savory tunes, and the broccoli rolls out those crisp, green beats. With every bite, it's a flavor symphony that's as satisfying as a perfect guitar riff in a rock ballad. So, get ready to groove with the ultimate rock 'n' roll of taste in every mouthful!

Ingredients

  • Beef (flank steak or sirloin), thinly sliced
  • Broccoli (florets)
  • Soy Sauce
  • Oyster Sauce
  • Cornstarch

Method

  1. Prepare the Beef:
    - Thinly slice the beef and marinate it in a mixture of soy sauce and cornstarch. Let it sit for around 15-20 minutes.
  2. Prepare the Broccoli:
    - While the beef is marinating, cut the broccoli into florets and blanch or steam them until they're crisp-tender. Set them aside.
  3. Stir-Fry:
    - Heat a pan or wok over medium-high heat. Add a small amount of oil.
    - Stir-fry the marinated beef in the heated pan until it's no longer pink. Remove the beef from the pan and set it aside.
  4. Combine and Thicken:
    - In the same pan, add the broccoli and a mix of soy sauce and oyster sauce. Stir-fry for a minute or two.
  5. Final Touches and Serving:
    - Mix the beef back into the pan with the broccoli.
    - Add a slurry of cornstarch and water to thicken the sauce. Stir until the sauce coats the beef and broccoli evenly.
  6. Serve:
    - Serve the Beef and Broccoli hot over steamed white rice.
